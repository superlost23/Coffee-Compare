Privacy

This site exists to compare specialty coffee prices. It collects the absolute minimum data needed to do that — nothing else.

What we don’t collect

  • No cookies. The site sets none.
  • No analytics scripts (no Google Analytics, no Plausible, no Fathom).
  • No IP addresses are stored. Web-server access logs are configured to drop them.
  • No user agents, browser fingerprints, or device IDs.
  • No accounts. There is no sign-up.

What we do log

To know which producers and varietals people are searching for, we record one row per search containing only:

  • The hour (not minute or second) the search happened
  • What kind of search it was (URL paste, producer, varietal, etc.)
  • A normalized form of the search text (lowercased, accents stripped)
  • Whether we found a match and the top score

If you paste a URL, the URL itself is not stored — only the structured fields we extract from it (producer, farm, varietal, process, origin).

Third parties

When we don’t already have a coffee’s data, we make HTTP requests to the roaster’s public website to fetch it. Those roasters may log our request, but the request comes from the server, not from your browser, and contains no information about you.

Cookies on outbound links

When you click through to a roaster’s site, that site has its own privacy policy and may set cookies. We’re not in a position to control that.

Contact

For privacy questions, email the contact address listed in our scraper’s User-Agent header (visible to roasters in their logs).
